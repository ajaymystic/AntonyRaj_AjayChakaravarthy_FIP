Rooms Summary

Whether you're celebrating love, rekindling connection, or simply taking a break from the world — our hand-crafted suites and villas offer the perfect setting for memorable moments. Discover the comfort, privacy, and romance that await you.

Slip into a world of serenity where every room is tailored for comfort, romance, and escape. From private balconies to warm fireside moments, let love lead the way.

Chateaus

The Chateaus are intimate luxury suites designed for couples seeking a romantic lakeside escape. With a warm European charm, each room offers a cozy wood-burning fireplace, plush furnishings, and serene views of the lake from a private balcony or deck. Ideal for honeymooners or couples celebrating a special occasion.

Read More

Villas

The Villas offer the perfect balance of comfort and elegance in a spacious open-concept layout. Designed with relaxation in mind, guests can enjoy a private hot tub on the balcony, curl up by the fireplace, or unwind in the luxurious two person tub. Perfect for longer stays or luxury getaways

Read More

Masters

The Masters suites deliver peak luxury with grand 10-foot ceilings and stunning lake views. These suites are all about indulgence—with steam showers, heated floors, double vanities, and even a separate bidet/toilet room. Furnished by The Art Shoppe of Toronto, they blend modern design with timeless romance.

Read More

Exotics

The Exotics rooms offer a bold and cozy retreat for couples looking for both comfort and privacy. Nestled by the water, these rooms include elegant spa-like features, a modern interior, and scenic views to set the mood. They’re a perfect pick for adventurous romantics.

Read More

Chalet

The Chalets are private, rustic luxury cabins offering an intimate lakeside retreat. Completely finished in dark cedar, these cabins provide a cozy ambiance that blends seamlessly with the natural surroundings. Guests can enjoy the soothing luxury of both an indoor whirlpool bathtub and an outdoor hot tub on a spacious sun deck

Read More

Junior Suite

The Junior Suites are designed to bring guests closer to nature with elements of real cedar wood, marble, and granite, reflecting the essence of Algonquin Park. Each suite features a private deck with an outdoor hot tub overlooking the waterfront, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the tranquil surroundings.
