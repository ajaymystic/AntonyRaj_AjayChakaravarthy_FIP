Rooms Summary

Escape to your own private retreat nestled by the lake — where elegant design meets warm, cozy comfort. The Chateaus offer an intimate setting perfect for couples, featuring private hot tubs, romantic fireplaces, and stunning waterfront views

The Chateaus are designed for the ultimate romantic getaway. These beautifully crafted suites feature luxurious interiors with a rustic luxe aesthetic — rich woods, warm tones, and large windows overlooking Galeairy Lake. Whether you're sipping wine by the fire, stargazing from your hot tub, or relaxing in a spa-style bathroom, the Chateaus are made for connection and comfort.
